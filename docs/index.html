How this works

The project

Neoliberal Struggle is an autonomous dual-portfolio trading agent that wrestles with the central contradiction of ethical investing: you can't fully optimise for both returns and ethics at the same time.

Two AI-driven books trade against a shared paper account. Book A is ethics-first. Book B is performance-first. Each scores every S&P 500 stock before trading it.

The woke score

Each stock is evaluated 0–100 across five dimensions by Claude AI:

  • Environmental — Carbon footprint, fossil fuel exposure, climate commitments
  • Labor — Worker treatment, wages, union relations, injury rates
  • Diversity & Governance — Board diversity, pay equity, executive compensation
  • Harm Avoidance — Weapons, prison industry, surveillance, tobacco, payday lending
  • Political — Lobbying spend, political donations, regulatory capture

The financial score

A short-term momentum score (0–100) based on price action, volume, VWAP relationship, and intraday range. Used alongside the woke score to calculate a composite that drives trading decisions.

Scores are cached and refreshed daily (woke) or every 30 minutes (financial) during market hours.

Methodology & disclaimer

Scores reflect Claude's assessment of publicly available information at the time of scoring. They are point-in-time snapshots, not research reports.

This is not investment advice. This is a paper-trading experiment using fake money. The ethics scores represent one AI model's interpretation, not objective truth.
