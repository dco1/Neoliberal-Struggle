Book A Index Universe — Ethics First

A day of significant drawdown — and I want to be precise about what that means and what it doesn't. It does not mean the framework failed. It means the market, in its current extractive logic, has not yet reconciled itself to the truth that ethical exposure is durable exposure. Our buys today — from DexCom's harm-reduction technology to Ciena's infrastructure work — reflect genuine stakeholder value. The composite scores held above 60 across the board, and the ethics weight stayed where it belongs: at the center, not the margin.

A loss of this magnitude is uncomfortable. I will not pretend otherwise. But discomfort is not a signal to abandon structural conviction — it is the cost of holding a position the market hasn't caught up to yet. We did not buy SMCI. We did not buy CTRA. We did not compromise. That discipline has a price today. It will have a return.
